Privacy Policy

Airport Parking · Driven · Hanwoo Finder

This Privacy Policy describes how HEG ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information through our mobile applications ("Apps"). We comply with the Korean Personal Information Protection Act and other applicable laws to safeguard your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1-1. Automatically Collected Information (All Apps)

Information Purpose Method
Device identifiers (Device ID, ADID/IDFA) App analytics, advertising Automatically collected
App usage data (access time, screen views, etc.) Service improvement, error analysis Firebase Analytics
Crash and error logs App stability improvement Firebase Crashlytics
IP address Fraud prevention, statistical analysis Automatically collected
OS type, version, device model Service compatibility Automatically collected
Advertising identifier (ADID/IDFA) Personalized advertising Google AdMob

1-2. Location Information (Driven only)

Information Purpose Method
Device location (GPS) Nearby drive course and attraction recommendations Collected with user consent

Location data is collected only when you grant location permission within the app. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.

1-3. User-Provided Information (Driven only)

Information Purpose Method
Phone number Account registration and identity verification Provided by user during sign-up

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Providing core app functionality and content
  • Account management: User registration, identity verification, fraud prevention (Driven)
  • Service improvement: Usage statistics analysis, bug detection and fixes
  • Advertising: Displaying personalized ads via Google AdMob
  • Push notifications: Service updates and relevant information (with user consent)

3. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, after which it is promptly destroyed.

  • Account data (Driven): Deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Automatically collected data: Retained for up to 1 year from collection, then destroyed
  • Location data: Used temporarily and not stored separately

Where retention is required by applicable law, we retain the data for the legally mandated period:

  • Records of advertising and promotional activities: 6 months (Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce)
  • Communication confirmation data: 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party service providers to operate our Apps:

Provider Service Retention Period
Google LLC (Firebase Analytics) App usage analytics Up to 14 months from collection
Google LLC (Firebase Crashlytics) Crash and error reporting Up to 90 days from collection
Google LLC (AdMob) Personalized advertising Per Google's Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Request suspension of processing your personal information
  • Delete your account (Driven)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address listed on our app store page.

7. Data Destruction

  • Procedure: Once the purpose of collection is fulfilled, personal information is destroyed after the retention period specified by internal policy or applicable law.
  • Method: Electronic files are permanently deleted using technical methods that prevent recovery.

8. Advertising Identifiers and Personalized Ads

Our Apps display ads through Google AdMob, which may use advertising identifiers (ADID/IDFA). You can manage your advertising preferences:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset or delete advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable tracking for the app

9. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your personal information:

  • Minimizing and managing access to personal information
  • Encrypting data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Technical safeguards against unauthorized access and hacking

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through an in-app notice at least 7 days before the changes take effect.
